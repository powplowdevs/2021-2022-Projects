Irish coffee macarons

If you haven’t had the courage to try making homemade macarons yet, this is the recipe for you! Here are some tips to begin with: When weighing ingredients, make sure to be as exact as possible and don’t skip the sifting of the dry ingredients, it’s an important step to give the macarons the correct texture. If you don’t have a macaron baking mat,, just use a pencil to mark equally-sized circles on a piece of parchment paper in advance to keep your macarons perfect.
